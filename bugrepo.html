🛠️ RailForge India Bug Reporting

Welcome to the official RailForge India Bug Reporting group! 🛠️

This is the dedicated space for our community to help us improve. Whether you've spotted a glitch on the RailForge India website or encountered an issue with one of our addons, we want to hear about it.

📋 How to Report:

  1. Identify: Is it a website error or an addon bug?
  2. Details: Provide a brief description of what happened.
  3. Evidence: Attach screenshots or screen recordings if possible.
  4. Specs: Mention your device or simulator version if relevant.

Your feedback helps us build a smoother experience for everyone. Thanks for helping us keep the tracks clear! 🚆✨
