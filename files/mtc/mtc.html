🚆 MTC Controller Addon

The MTC Controller Addon is the core system that powers your train addons, allowing you to control and operate trains smoothly in Minecraft.

This addon provides essential tools and mechanics required for running locomotives and coaches, making your railway system fully functional and interactive.

🔹 Features:

  • Advanced train control system
  • Includes railway tracks for building routes
  • Easy-to-use train controller interface
  • Smooth and realistic train movement
  • Supports multiple train addons

Use this addon as the backbone of your railway world and bring full control to your trains with ease.
