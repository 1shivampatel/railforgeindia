🚆 Route: Chenab Valley Minecraft Rail Route | Reasi ➝ Dugga 🌉

Experience one of the most breathtaking railway stretches, now built in Minecraft with incredible realism — the Reasi to Dugga route, featuring the iconic Chenab Rail Bridge, the world’s highest railway arch bridge standing at an altitude of ~359 meters above the river.

🏔️ This route captures the beauty of the Himalayan terrain with detailed mountains, deep valleys, tunnels, and realistic track alignment.
🗺️ The entire project is accurately recreated using Google Maps references, ensuring true-to-life geography and proportions.

🚉 Stations Included:

  • Reasi – Scenic starting point
  • Bakkal – Midway halt near the bridge
  • Dugga – Final destination in the hills

🌉 Witness the stunning Chenab Bridge crossing, recreated block by block in Minecraft, delivering a truly immersive experience.

✨ A perfect combination of creativity and engineering, bringing India’s greatest railway marvel into the Minecraft world.
