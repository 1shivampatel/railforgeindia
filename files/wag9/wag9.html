Overview

Experience the true power of the Indian Railways right inside your Minecraft world! The WAG-9 Electric Locomotive Addon, crafted with high-fidelity detail by RailForge India, brings the workhorse of India's freight lines into Minecraft Bedrock and Pocket Edition. Known for hauling massive cargo loads across the country, this iconic 3-phase heavy-haul beast is fully operational, perfectly scaled, and ready to dominate your custom rail networks.

Key Features

🔹 Authentic Indian Railways Design

  • True-to-Life Scale: Built using precise proportions modeled after the actual real-world WAG-9 dimensions.
  • Signature Livery: Featuring the classic iconic green and yellow livery variant synonymous with Indian freight operations.
  • Intricate Roof Gear: Detailed high-quality modeling of the pantographs, insulators, and roof-mounted equipment lines.

🔹 Advanced Technical Optimization

  • Smooth Movement: Engineered for seamless, lag-free performance across complex tracks and junctions.
  • MTC Integration: Fully compatible with the Train Control System (MTC) for an authentic signal and operation experience.
  • Dual-Direction Visibility: High-quality windshield assets ensure perfect visibility whether operating from Cab 1 or Cab 2.

🔹 Immersive Gameplay

  • Heavy-Haul Capability: Engineered to pull extensive lines of cargo, box, or container wagons.
  • Realistic Texturing: Tailored textures that respect the blocky world while retaining industrial realism.
  • Multiplayer Ready: Fully optimized for survival or creative roleplay servers and cooperative rail maps.

How to Install and Use

  1. Download: Obtain the latest .mcaddon file from our official channels.
  2. Import: Open the file to automatically import it into Minecraft Bedrock/PE.
  3. Activate: Enable both the Resource Pack and Behavior Pack in your world settings.
  4. Experiments: Ensure Experimental Gameplay options (like Holiday Creator Features) are toggled ON in your world settings for maximum functionality.
  5. Spawn: Find the locomotive in your Creative inventory under spawn eggs, or place it down using MTC system rails.
