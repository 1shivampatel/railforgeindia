About Us

RAILFORGE INDIA is a passionate team of creators dedicated to bringing realistic Indian railway content into Minecraft. We focus on creating high-quality locomotives, detailed interiors, and immersive train systems for players who love trains and creativity.

Our goal is to recreate the feel of Indian Railways with accuracy, performance, and fun gameplay. From powerful diesel engines to detailed cabins, every addon is crafted with attention to detail.
